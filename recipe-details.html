Ultimate Veggie Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 8 ounces cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Kosher salt and black pepper
  • 3 cups shredded low-moisture part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup canned artichoke hearts, chopped
  • ½ cup roasted red bell peppers, sliced
  • ½ cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup black olives, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves, crushed red pepper flakes, and grated Parmesan cheesefor garnish

Instruction:

  1. Preheat the oven to 500°F and place a pizza stone inside.
  2. Divide the pizza dough into two pieces and let it rest on a floured surface.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet, cook mushrooms until tender, and stir in garlic. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Stretch the dough into a 10-12 inch circle and spread pizza sauce, leaving a 1 inch border.
  5. Add mozzarella cheese and toppings like mushrooms, artichokes, peppers, onions, and oviles.
  6. Tranfer the pizza onto the hot pizza stone and bake for 8-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  7. Garnish with basil, red pepper flakes, and paemesan cheese before serving.

Silky Garlic Chive Aushak
(Afghan Dumplings)

Ingredients:

  • 1 Pack dumplings wrappers
  • 1 cup garlic chives, chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp dried mint

Instruction:

  1. Fill dumplings wrappers with chopped garlic chives and seal them tightly.
  2. Boils water and cook dumplings for about 5 minutes.
  3. mix yogurt with dried mint for the sauce.
  4. Heat tomato sauce with spices and pour over dumplings.
  5. Serve with yogurt sauce and garnish with fresh herbs.

Classic Oreo Cookie Shake
(Creamy & Indulgent)

Ingredients:

  • 6 Oreo cookies
  • 2 cups chilled milk
  • scoops vanilla ice cream
  • tbsp chocolate syrup (optional)
  • Whipped cream for topping (optional)
  • Extra crushed Oreos for garnish

Instruction:

  1. Crush Oreo cookies and add them to a blender.
  2. Pour in the chilled milk, vanilla ice cream, and chocolate syrup (if using).
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass and top with whipped cream and crushed Oreos.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy this rich, chocolatey shake!

Crispy Gloden Bolani
(Afghan Stuffed Flatbread)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 2 tbsp chopped cilantro
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Instruction:

  1. Mix flour with water to form a soft dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Combine mashed potatoes with spices and herbs to create the filling.
  3. Roll out the dough into thin circles and spread the filling evenly.
  4. Fold and seal the edges, then pan-fry with oil until crispy and golden brown.
  5. Serve with yogurt or chutney.

Classic saffron kulfi
(Frozen ice cream)

Ingredients:

  • 2 Cups full-Fat milk
  • 1/2 cup condensed mil
  • 1/4 tsp saffron threads
  • 1 tbsp chopped pistachios
  • 1 tbsp sugar

Instruction:

  1. heat the milk and simmer until reduce by half.
  2. add saffron, condensed mil, and sugar.
  3. Stir and let cool before pouring into molds.
  4. freeze for at least 6 hours, garnish with pistachios, and serve.

Vegan Panna Cotta

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, blackberries)
  • 2 tbsp sugar or honey
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice or orange juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instruction:

  1. Wash and gently pat dry all the fresh berries. Slice strawberries into halves or quarters if needed.
  2. Place the berries in a large mixing bowl. Sprinkle sugar or drizzle honey over them.
  3. Add lemon or orange juice and vanilla extract (optional) to the bowl. Toss the berries gently to ensure everything is evenly coated.
  4. Cover the bowl and allow the mixture to sit at room tempreture for 15-30 minutes.
    during the time, the berries will release their juice and absorb the flavors.
  5. Before serving, garnishwith fresh mint leaves for decorative touch and added freshness.

Peanut Butter Energy Balls

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup peanut butter
  • ¼ cup honey
  • ¼ cup mini chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons ground flaxseed

Instruction:

  1. Combine oats, peanut butter, honey, chocolate chips, and flaxseed in a bowl.
  2. Mix until the ingredients are well combined.
  3. Roll the mixture into bite-sizes balls using your hands.
  4. Refrigerate for 30 minutes to set.
  5. Enjoy!

Peach and Iced tea

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe peaches (3 chopped, 1 sliced for garnish)
  • 4 black tea bags
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • Ice cubes

Instruction:

  1. Boil 2 cups of water and steep the tea bags for 5 minutes. Remove the tea bags and let tea cool.
  2. In saucepan, combine chopped peaches, sugar, and 1 cup water. simmer until the peaches are soft, then mash them to realease their juices.
  3. Strain teh peach mixture through a fine sieve to make peach syrup.
  4. Combine the cooled tea, peach syrup, and 1 cup of cold water in a pitcher, Stir well.
  5. Serve over ice and garnish with peach slices.
